Transport To and From Catalina Island

Avalon | Two Harbors

Posted by Katherine Paris on December 7, 2023

If you plan to start the hike from Avalon to Two Harbors or the opposite way, these factors must be considered to be transported to the island. There are two major means to get to Catalina Island: either through the Catalina Express or the Catalina Flyer.

The Catalina Express

The Catalina Express has ferries that run from Dana Point, Long Beach, and San Pedro to and from Avalon. They also offer transportation from San Pedro to and from Two Harbors.

Reservations can be made through their website and are also available for purchase day of through at their ports.

The Catalina Flyer

The Catalina Flyer provides transport from Newport Beach to and from Avalon. Reservations are made available through their website
